Story

STORY

FREETAE.Deonte Edwards — producer, artist, and CEO of TierOneRecords, born & raised in Long Beach, California with a mindset that’s lived, not advertised:

TRIAL AND ERROR is more than just words — it’s a lifestyle.

I started making music in 2009 with nothing but hunger and belief. No shortcuts. No safety net. Just vision. That grind led me into collaborations and industry intersections with Lil Wayne, Iggy Azalea, Jeremih, Sean Kingston, Hit-Boy, King Combs, and Tracy T, with affiliations connected to Roc Nation and Young Money Records in partnership with Republic Records.

In 2016, I lost my mother and turned grief into drive. The true cost wasn’t tragedy — it was the years invested chasing something greater than myself. Pouring everything into that pursuit eventually created instability, and I experienced a brief season of homelessness. Determined to rebuild and regain control of my life, I stepped into hustling — a choice that resulted in a federal drug indictment in 2022. I am currently serving a federal sentence.

From confinement, the blueprint for TierOneRecords continues to expand with clarity and intention. Circumstances do not dictate direction. Pressure creates growth. The mission remains locked.

Built from the ground up.

THE JOURNEY

Long Beach, CA
Where It All Started
Born and raised in Long Beach, California. The foundation was built early — discipline, ambition, and a love for music that would define everything to come.
2009 — 2013
First Beats. First Bars.
Started making music with nothing but hunger and belief. No shortcuts, no safety net — just raw vision and a relentless work ethic. By 2013, the studio became home. Professional setups, real sessions, the foundation being built.
2014 — 2016
Industry Breakthrough
The years everything clicked. 2014 brought collaborations with Iggy Azalea, Nipsey Hussle, and sessions at Revolt Studios. By 2016, working at 17 Hertz Studio in North Hollywood — major studio, major moves. The transition from hungry newcomer to industry presence.
2018
TierOneRecords Founded
Built the label from the ground up — TierOneRecords became the vehicle for independence. No middlemen. Full creative and business control. Self-funded, self-owned.
2019 — 2022
The Catalog
Released IN DUE TIME, DAMNThatsCHILLEE, QUARTER MILL, and built a catalog of records blending commercial awareness with personal substance. Every release carried the same standard — quality over quantity.
2020
Peak Success
The fruits of the grind. Lamborghini in the driveway. The vision manifesting in real time. This was the peak before the storm — success earned through years of relentless work, but also the lifestyle that would eventually lead to consequences.
2025
Resilience & Focus
Serving a federal sentence, but the vision never stopped. From confinement, the blueprint for TierOneRecords continues to expand with clarity and intention. Circumstances do not dictate direction. Pressure creates growth.
2026
SIGN LANGUAGE
The debut single. Written, produced, and engineered by Deonte Edwards. Releasing July 17, 2026. The next chapter begins — and the mission remains locked.
